What is a Pug?

Pugs are playful, affectionate, and known for their charming personalities. They have a distinctive appearance with a short muzzle, large round eyes, and a curled tail. Pugs are great companions and adapt well to various living situations, making them popular pets worldwide.

Fun Fact

Pugs have been around before 400 B.C. They originated in ancient China. It is believed that monks bred them for their acute personalities.
